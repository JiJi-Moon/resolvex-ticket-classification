Platform Features

ResolveX combines AI-driven automation with structured workflow governance to deliver intelligent and scalable IT ticket processing.

🤖 AI Intelligence Engine

Automated Ticket Creation

Converts unstructured user input from chat, email, or web forms into structured ticket fields including title, description, department classification, and priority.

Intelligent Categorization

Uses NLP-based classification models to accurately assign tickets to predefined departments such as Hardware, Software, Network, or Application Support.

Named Entity Recognition (NER)

Extracts key entities including usernames, machine names, affected services, and error codes from free-form input.

Hybrid Priority Detection

Combines rule-based keyword analysis with machine learning models to determine urgency and impact level.

🔁 Workflow & Governance

Structured Lifecycle Management

Enforces defined ticket state transitions such as Open → In Progress → Resolved → Closed to ensure operational consistency and accountability.

Role-Based Access Control

Distinct user and administrator permissions provide secure and controlled workflow operations.

Resolution Confirmation Loop

Users confirm resolution or reopen tickets, ensuring transparency and feedback-driven closure.

Controlled Direct Closure

Administrators can directly close tickets with mandatory closure reasons to maintain governance.

📈 Business Impact

Reduced Triage Time

Automates the initial ticket processing stage, significantly decreasing manual workload.

Improved Accuracy

Minimizes misrouting and incorrect priority assignment, enhancing operational efficiency.

Scalable Support

Enables efficient handling of growing support volumes without proportional manpower increase.
